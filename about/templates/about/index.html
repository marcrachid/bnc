WE ARE THINK AHEAD

BNC (Bobaomby Nature Conservation) is a Non-profit organization registered in Madagascar which is working in the province of Antsiranana, the northern tip of Madagascar. Our work with local communities is focusing on the small community of the Key Biodiversity area of Ampombofofo on CONSERVATION and ECOTOURISM.

VISION

Protect the Environment with the consideration of the well-being of the local people

MISSION

Develop a community ecotourism activity that is beneficial for the Conservation of Environment and for the improvement of the communities’ life standards

INFRASTRUCTURE

ACCOMMODATION

Faithful to the philosophy of community eco-tourism. The tents well sheltered under a Hangard in our camp in the heart of the village. It is a place of discovery and enrichment. Contemplate this landscape rich in biodiversity and its sumptuous sunset beyond the village. And evenings animated by folk music.

RESTORATION

Every morning, hearty breakfasts will give you the catch for the day: hot drinks, pancakes, French toast, fruit plates, and Malagasy-style breakfasts. Lunch with our menus typical of the region and modern cuisine, at the camp or picnic during the visits. Composed of a starter, a main course and a dessert. And every evening, meals full of flavors and delicacies to awaken your taste buds.

WATER, TOILET AND WC

Respecting the standards, in hard, English chair, with the fresh air of the forest. Spacious shower.

ELECTRICITY

We respect the concept and the way of life of the villagers, using clean energy, which is solar panels. Just for charging cameras, computers, and phones, and lights.

ACCESSIBILITY

As in many tourist sites in Madagascar, the accessibility condition at the site level is not always easy, especially during the rainy season. That being said, potential visitors to this site will therefore be passionate about hiking. The site is open year round. From December to March it is the rainy season, there is a little mud, but the whole part is covered with sand, and makes the track easily accessible. Good time for research and studies, as well as village integration.

From April to November is the dry season, access is easy, and a good time for all kinds of activities. from Diego by boat for 1 hour 30 minutes to Ambanilanahely village, and a pleasant 1 hour hike to Anjiabe village. A journey by car or quad, is 5 hours to the village of Anjiabe.

TEMPERATURE

During the varatraza season, it is cool, because there is wind (Monsoon) with a daytime temperature between 27 ° to 30 ° C, and in the evening, we have a temperature between 22 ° to 25 ° vs. During the rainy season, it is hot and humid, and the temperature varies between 30 ° to 34 ° C.
