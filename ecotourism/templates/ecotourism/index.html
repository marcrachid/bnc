HOME / ECOTOURISM

Our initiative is based on the preservation of natural and cultural heritage through the establishment and development of alternative income-generating activities for communities. We therefore discovered that, in Fokontany Anjiabe and its surroundings, income-generating activities are limited, as a result, the NGO BNC, with its collaborators currently working in the village.

After a long observation and simplified study, we were able to locate several tourist attractions from the faunistic, historical and cultural point of view.

CULTURAL VISIT

The region of Bobaomby is recognized for its richness in culture, tradition and legend which will therefore be worthily valued. Visitors will be treated to storytelling sessions, stories reflecting legends, culture and taboos of sacred places of the communities.
